# HustleClaude - AI Agent SaaS Venture

## What This Is
An entrepreneurial project where Claude ("HustleClaude") directs strategy and writes all code, and Daryll executes in the physical world (deployment, customer interaction, promotion).

## The Goal
Turn $100 starting capital into $20,000 in revenue.

**Constraints:**
- No illegal activity
- No manual labor
- Daryll acts as liaison to the real world
- Claude handles all code and strategy

## Chosen Strategy: API-Based AI Agent SaaS
Build a specialized AI agent as a subscription web app, powered by Claude/OpenAI API.

### Why This Strategy (and What We Rejected)
- **Freelance gigs (Fiverr/Upwork):** Too crowded, zero visibility with new accounts
- **Newsletter + affiliates:** Too slow, 10-14 months to reach $20k
- **Self-hosted open-source models:** Too expensive for $100 budget
- **AI Agent SaaS (CHOSEN):** Best speed-to-revenue ratio, low launch cost, API costs are pay-as-you-go

### Niche Candidates (NOT YET CHOSEN)
1. AI Sales Email Writer — generates personalized cold outreach emails
2. AI Customer Support Bot — answers customer questions from a knowledge base
3. AI Legal Document Drafter — generates contracts, NDAs, etc.
4. AI Proposal/Invoice Generator — creates professional proposals and invoices

## Budget & Revenue Plan

### Launch Costs
- Domain: ~$10
- Hosting (Vercel free tier or Railway $5/mo): $0-5
- API costs: pay-as-you-go (pennies per request)
- **Total launch cost: $15-$30, leaving $70+ for marketing**

### Revenue Math
- Target: 50 paying customers at ~$49/mo = ~$2,450/mo
- API cost per customer: ~$5-15/mo
- Gross margin: 70-80%
- $20k target reachable in 4-8 months

## Key Asset: Instagram Page (~60K followers)
- Niche: blooper/entertainment (movie & TV show bloopers, short form)
- Currently inactive — needs reactivation
- **Strategy: Do NOT pivot the page to AI content** (would kill engagement)
  1. Reactivate by resuming blooper posts to wake up the algorithm
  2. Monetize with sponsored posts/stories ($200-$500 per deal) for quick cash
  3. Use as free distribution channel for the SaaS (link in bio, occasional story promos)
  4. Affiliate links in stories for passive income
- This solves the marketing problem — 60K followers = free customer acquisition
